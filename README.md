## Contributor License Agreement (CLA)  
Before making a contribution to the PowerShell project, you must agree to our Contributor License Agreement (CLA). If you're making submissions as an individual and you hold the rights to your submissions, please reply with:  
```
@microsoft-github-policy-service agree
```  
If your submissions are made in the course of your work for your employer or your employer has intellectual property rights to your submissions, please reply with:  
```
@microsoft-github-policy-service agree company="{Your Company}"
```  
Make sure to replace `{Your Company}` with the name of your employer if applicable.
